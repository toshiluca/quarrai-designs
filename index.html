Quarrai — design concepts

Round 2 — synthesis concepts. Built from the July 10 review: the concept-3/5/9 blend, the public-homepage vs. daily-page split, podcasts front and center, the credit tracker, and the community model. These are interactive — tabs, search, toggles, and demos work.

Concept 11 · New

Homebase

The public homepage: scrolling article snapshots up top, big search with working Trending / Featured / Podcasts / Gems tabs, a podcast band with a player, color-coded pillars, and the provider premium-listing strip.

Try: the tabs, the search box, the podcast play button.
Concept 12 · New

My Quarrai

The member’s daily page — “here’s your day at a glance.” Widget-based and user-customizable, with an Analyst vs. Executive view toggle (gamification for rank-and-file, snapshot-only for senior people).

Try: the view toggle and “Customize my page.”
Concept 13 · New

Tracker

The credential command center: every certification’s requirements and progress in one place, listen-and-attest podcast credits (the medical-CME model), a goal-driven learning-path generator, and a certifications comparison directory.

Try: “Earn 0.5” on the podcast, and “blockchain forensics” in the path builder.
Concept 14 · New

The Exchange

Community Q&A: ask a question, get an instant AI first answer, community rates usefulness, verified experts settle it. Leaderboards that say top contributor of what, expert reputation by answer ratings, and a careers teaser.

Try: asking a question, voting, and the leaderboard tabs.

Round 1 — the original ten

Ten distinct layouts for the same platform, using the real brand (Inter type, the mining/“goldmine” voice, the two pillars, and your content sections). Click any to open it full-screen. Same content, ten very different personalities.

Concept 1

Clarity

Clean modern SaaS. Centered hero + search, two big side-by-side pillar cards (Knowledge / Learning), social-proof stats. Professional, calm, conversion-focused.

Best if: you want a trustworthy, mainstream B2B feel.
Concept 2

Daily Dig

Editorial “compliance newspaper” dashboard. Masthead, three-column news layout, left topic rail, right leaderboard + provider ad. Dense, authoritative, daily-habit.

Best if: the news/knowledge pillar leads the product.
Concept 3

Quarry

Bold, brand-forward, playful. Cream background, colorful brushstroke energy, big mining-themed headers, chunky rounded cards. Leans hard into the Quarrai personality.

Best if: you want to stand out from grey compliance sites.
Concept 4

Vault

Dark, app-style logged-in dashboard. Sidebar nav, points/level/streak widgets, leaderboard, badges, “continue learning.” Shows the gamified product itself.

Best if: you want to showcase the member experience.
Concept 5

Goldmine

Search-first minimalism. One giant central search bar (“your information goldmine”), tabbed by content type, quick filters, sections below the fold. Google-simple.

Best if: discovery/search is the core action.
Concept 6

Split

Full-screen duotone split — Knowledge on one side, Learning on the other, with a “Know & Learn” seam in the middle and a shared search bar. Bold and instantly explains the two pillars.

Best if: you want the dual concept understood in one glance.
Concept 7

Editorial

Premium magazine feel — big serif display type, airy asymmetric grid, numbered articles. Upscale and authoritative, more “publication” than “app.”

Best if: you want a prestigious, thought-leadership tone.
Concept 8

Terminal

Bloomberg-style “compliance intelligence.” Live regulatory ticker, monospace command search, dense data panels. Signals a serious, real-time professional tool.

Best if: you want to feel like premium market-data software.
Concept 9

Community

Warm and human — member avatars, star testimonials, pastel feature cards, badges front and center. Leads with social proof and the people using it.

Best if: community & trust are your growth engine.
Concept 10

Aurora

Modern fintech/startup look — dark aurora gradient background, frosted glass cards, glowing gradient headline. Sleek, contemporary, and eye-catching.

Best if: you want a cutting-edge, of-the-moment vibe.
